The Grand is a short work of experimental interactive fiction, designed to allow readers to navigate the physical space of a seafront art deco hotel, from several different perspectives. You can switch viewpoint at any time, inhabiting the same rooms and locations at different times, and from different perspectives.

When you begin, you'll be asked to make a choice. You can change this at any time as you explore the work. When you change viewpoint, your physical position in The Grand will not change, but your character and the time and context they inhabit will.
